Come and join us in this Case Study Competition and challenge your abilities to prepare innovative solutions to exciting real-life problems and get to win a cash prize of ₹ 10,000.

Participation Rule:

  • Any team of students enrolled in any academic institution can participate in this challenge.
  • Team Size: Maximum 4 members in each team

    Code The Problem

    Can you fuse your knowledge of chemical engineering subjects and your coding skills to solve exciting problems? Join us to prove your mettle and win a cash prize of ₹10,000.

    This event consists of three rounds - one preliminary round and two eliminative out-rounds. In each round, the participants will be given a problem related to chemical engineering which they must solve using their code.

    Participation Rule:

  • Any team of students enrolled in any academic institution can participate in this challenge.
  • Team Size: Maximum 3 members in each team
  • Cross-Institute teams are allowed.
  • Language Constraints: You may use C, C++, Java, Python or MATLAB.

    • Registration Deadline: 25th January 2022

    The problem statement for the preliminary round will be sent to the participating teams through an email on 25th January 2022.
